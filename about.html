✦ Our Story

India's Most Trusted Railing Manufacturer

Founded over 15 years ago, RailingWorld began with a simple vision — to bring world-class railing design and manufacturing to Indian homes, offices and commercial spaces.

We are a perfectionist in introducing uniquely designed Aluminum glass railing, Modular railing, Balcony railing, Stainless steel balcony railing, Glass stair railing, Glass deck railing and many more. Each piece in our portfolio showcases the creativity of our designers who pour their imagination into crafting outstanding railings.

Widely accepted for introducing matchless quality and designer arrays of railing in Aluminum, Stainless Steel and Glass — RailingWorld is the name architects, builders, and homeowners trust.

✦ Our Journey

Milestones & Growth

2008
Company Founded
Started as a small fabrication workshop in Mumbai with a focus on quality aluminum railings.
2012
Glass Railing Division Launched
Expanded into glass and stainless steel railings — serving premium residential projects across Maharashtra.
2016
State-of-the-Art Factory
Moved to a larger manufacturing plant with CNC machinery and dedicated QC lab. Production capacity tripled.
2020
Pan-India Expansion
Established dealership networks in 10+ states. Completed 2000+ projects across India.
2025
5000+ Projects Milestone
Crossed 5000 successful projects. Trusted by top architects, builders, and real estate developers nationwide.
